Contents of services

Cloud-ID system supports flexible combination of all function modules according to the requirements of the event, but you don't need to study the system operation, and we will send professional project team, which can provide the whole-process support services for each project.


At the conference/event planning stage, you can contact us (see contact information below the page), and obtain professional guest management platform with suggestions and advice.

We provide the following additional services to simplify your planning and management work. Let us help you take good care of the guest in the events!

Equipment leasing

Including iPAD, Nexus series tablets and PC required for Cloud-ID system; RFID/NFC wireless hand scanning hot spots; network sets and card printing equipment, etc.
Provide all equipment with logistics service, so you don’t need to worry about transportation.

* Different Cloud - ID system function modules need different hardware equipment, please contact us for professional advice.

Supplies customization

Provide RFID card customization, RFID special-shaped CARDS make, chip card customization, NFC tags (bracelet, wrist strap, etc.) or label customization, 3G ExpressCard and card-printer cartridges, etc.

* Prepare different consumption materials according to different requirements of different projects. Please contact us for professional advice.Please note that you reserve at least 20 working days for making consumption materials so as to avoid the extra urgent cost.

Interface customization

Cloud-ID system supports the flexible replacement of terminal UI. You can design your proprietary UI for the event on your own or by entrusting us. We will integrate and update them to the tablets or big screen terminal.
Even you can also integrate the conference documents and materials in electronic copy into the exclusive tablet app so as to save the cost in paper and printing. It is environmentally friendly and with a sense of science and technology.

* We recommend that you reserve at least 15 working days for UI design and additional 5 working days for program integration.

Concierge car

We provide car services for all kinds of conferences and events including VIP guests concierge cars, bus, truck for goods and airport transportation service, short-distance shuffle services, etc.

* This service is provided by our partners and settled all by us. All of drivers and conductors are well trained to use Cloud-ID and also be able to take charge of jobs of guest check-in, gift distribution and information collection, etc.

The security service

Provide on-site security personnel, “Men in Black” personal body guards for VIP guests.

* This service is provided by our partners and settled all by us.The security persons are well trained to use Cloud-ID and also be able to take charge of guest check-in, gift distribution, information collection and guest guidance etc.

The etiquette service

Provide on-site professional etiquette staff to deal with guest guide work.

* This service is provided by our partners and settled all by us.The etiquette staff are well trained to use Cloud-ID and also be able to take charge of guest check-in, gift distribution, information collection and guest guidance etc.

Data services

Provide customized chart production, the production of reports and data mining services.
