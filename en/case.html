  • Project：Estee Lauder Lady Salon city tour

    Project：Estee Lauder Lady Salon city tour

    Project background :

           Every year Estee Lauder provides a lady salon's tour season, and in different cities it will invite Estee Lauder members to participate in the event of beautiful skin care salon.
           Sponsors need to update the latest information of new members and maintain their brand loyalty (gifts and small samples issued) through these events while providing VIP guests with free skin index test and tracking records.    
           In previous years, all the invitations, tracking, records, etc. are conducted through a traditional form of paper and pencil, it is inefficient and very easy to make mistakes, and it often aroused the dissatisfaction from members, and virtually reduced the brand image.
           Therefore, Estee Lauder started to use Cloud-ID system in the Lady Salon Tour in the summer of 2012 up to now.


    Cloud-ID solutions:
           Based on the above requirements of the Sponsor and combined with the background of the event, we finally have implemented the following solutions:
              1. Make web page for invitation. Marketing staff only needs to send the web site to the members, and the members just log in to the web page to fill in the receipt. The system automatically deals with the registration guests list;
              2. With iPAD ‘pilot-style’ smart Check-in system, the guests who have already filled in the receipts can just check in by the name index, and the guests who have not filled in the receipts only need to check in with iPAD which will be switched to the on-site registration mode, and the guests information for those registered on site is automatically uploaded to the guests database through synchronization. 
              3. Customize event theme iPAD app UI. iPAD app integrates the electronic color page of the latest product introduction
              4. iPAD app also provides the administration mode to record and check the gift distribution;
              5. If the system has identified VIP members, it provides additional skin test data record page, and the recorded result is automatically uploaded to the "Cloud" database;
              6. Generate the summary report after the event, and synchronize the event data to the guests member database so as to complete the automatic updating of members information; 
              7. Remote engineers' technical services for the whole process of the event.
     * The solution has been applied in "Lady Salon City Tour" in the year of 2012 and 2013.

    Project：HSBC 2013 Sheshan International Golf Tour Audience-interactive Experience Area

    Project background :

           As the main sponsor of International Golf Tour, HSBC bank (HSBC) hopes to promote its latest financial products to the audiences who come to watch the game by setting the interactive exhibition area in the game area, while recording the potential customer information and providing the follow-up services for business people.  
           The challenge for the project are: how to attract the audiences into the interactive display area?
           And what kind of way will be used to show product information, and record the customer information?

    Cloud-ID solutions:
           Based on the above requirements of the Sponsor and combined with the background of the event, we finally have implemented the following solutions:

               1. Customize RFID card in the shape of golf, with exquisite wrist strap to fit the background of events. Distribute that RFID card to the audiences as souvenirs when they have entered the interactive display area   
               2.Play product video on-site through HDTV, and install RFID scanning hot spots below each HDTV. Only after the audience have scanned their RFID card, can it be possible to switch among the different product videos. 
               3.Install iPAD terminal on site. Audiences can fill in questionnaire after scanning their RFID card, and they can get additional gifts when they completing the questionnaire.
               4.Check whether the audiences have completed the questionnaire by using RFID scanning hot spots at the gift pick-up place.
               5.Use an AR interactive game to attract more audiences into the interactive display area (AR customization development services provided by Zealion, see http://iPLUS.zealion.com).
               6. Provide engineer on-site support in the whole process. 
               7. Record the behavior information of each audience in the display area (what videos has he watched, and the situations of prizes received, etc.) By using RFID technology, which will be automatically associated to the questionnaire information (including personal information) in the database.Data was imported after the event and send to the business department for follow-up.
